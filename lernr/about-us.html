Our Vision

We believe that good teachers are the soul and strength of the education system. India is facing a major crisis in quality of education. The number of students is rising much faster than the number of good teachers. So India needs a platform where good teachers are able to connect with more and more students.

We are building the largest verifiable network of good teachers in India which will be freely available to students and parents.

Our vision is to enable every student to learn from a good teacher.
